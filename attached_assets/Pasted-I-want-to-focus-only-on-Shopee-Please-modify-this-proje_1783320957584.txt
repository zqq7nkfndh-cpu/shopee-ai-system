I want to focus only on Shopee.

Please modify this project into a Shopee-only AI product research and listing system.

Goal:
The app should research potentially profitable Japanese products for Shopee cross-border selling, create listing drafts, calculate profit, check risks, and eventually list approved products to Shopee using the official Shopee Open Platform API.

Important safety rules:
- Remove or hide Rakuten ROOM features.
- Do not auto-post to Rakuten ROOM.
- Shopee auto-listing must be disabled by default.
- Add a dry-run mode first.
- Only products with approved = true can be exported or listed.
- Do not list high-risk products.
- Do not list food, supplements, cosmetics, batteries, liquids, medicine, counterfeit goods, branded goods with unclear rights, copyrighted character goods, or prohibited items unless manually confirmed.
- Final listing should require a clear manual confirmation button.
